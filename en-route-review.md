# Review and complete
{: #en-route-review}

Review your routing configuration and test the event flow to ensure notifications are delivered correctly.
{: shortdesc}

## Reviewing your configuration
{: #en-route-review-config}

1. On the **Review** step, verify the following:
   - Topic name and description
   - Event filter details
   - Subscription details

2. Click **Done** to complete the review.

Your topic is now active with the configured filters and subscriptions. Events that match your filter conditions are routed to the configured destinations.

You can edit the configuration at any time by clicking **Back** on the **Review** step, or by navigating to the topic from the Topics page.

## Next steps
{: #en-route-review-next}

Now that you set up a complete event routing flow, you can:

* [Create notification templates](/docs/event-notifications?topic=event-notifications-en-create-en-template) to customize the format and content of your notifications.

* [Monitor notification delivery](/docs/event-notifications?topic=event-notifications-en-monitoring) to track performance and troubleshoot issues.
